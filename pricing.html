Frequently Asked Questions

What does the free plan include?

The free plan gives you full access to our flight and hotel search engines, destination guides, and travel blog. You can search and compare prices from hundreds of providers at no cost.

What is the AI travel concierge?

The AI concierge is your personal travel planner. Chat with it about your dream trip and it will create complete, personalized itineraries with flight options, hotel recommendations, restaurant suggestions, and unique experiences — all tailored to your budget and preferences.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

When you sign up for Pro, you get 7 days of full access to the AI concierge for free. You can cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be charged. After 7 days, you'll be billed $4.99/month.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your Stripe billing portal. There are no contracts, no cancellation fees, and no questions asked.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment partner Stripe. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

Is the concierge available in my language?

Yes! Our AI concierge speaks multiple languages. Simply write to it in your preferred language and it will respond accordingly. It supports English, Italian, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and many more.
