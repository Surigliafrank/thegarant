Hotel Booking Tips

🌟 Check Reviews

Always read recent reviews. Look for patterns — one bad review is fine, but recurring complaints are a red flag.

📍 Location Matters

A cheaper hotel far from the center can cost more in transport. Factor in total costs, not just the room rate.

💳 Book Direct Sometimes

After finding a hotel, check the hotel's own website. They sometimes offer better rates or perks for direct bookings.

🍔 Breakfast Included?

Hotels with breakfast included can save you $10-20 per person daily. Factor this into your price comparison.
