Italy

Rome

The Eternal City blends ancient wonders with vibrant modern life. Explore the Colosseum, toss a coin in the Trevi Fountain, and savor authentic pasta in Trastevere. Rome offers history, art, and gastronomy at every corner.

Best time to visit: April to June, September to October

Average hotel price: $120 - $300 per night

Barcelona

Barcelona dazzles with Gaudi's masterpieces, sun-kissed beaches, and a legendary food scene. Walk down Las Ramblas, marvel at the Sagrada Familia, and enjoy tapas by the Mediterranean Sea.

Best time to visit: May to June, September to October

Average hotel price: $100 - $250 per night
