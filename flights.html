Flight Booking Tips

📅 Book 2-4 Months Ahead

The sweet spot for the best flight prices is 2-4 months before departure for international flights.

📈 Fly Midweek

Tuesday and Wednesday flights are typically 15-30% cheaper than weekend departures.

✈ Consider Open-Jaw

Fly into one city and out of another to save money and avoid backtracking on multi-city trips.

💰 Use Price Alerts

Set up price alerts for your route and get notified when prices drop to grab the best deal.
